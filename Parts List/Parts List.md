🛠️ Required Parts

The following components are required:

 - ESP32-S3 Display with 2.8-inch, 240 × 320 pixel IPS capacitive touchscreen, 512 kB SRAM, 16 MB Flash, and 8 MB PSRAM.

   The board specifications must match these requirements to ensure the software runs properly. I used this one:

   https://www.amazon.de/FREENOVE-Capacitive-Supporting-XiaoZhiAI-Microcontroller/dp/B0FSQF6FKN?th=1
   or https://de.aliexpress.com/item/1005009876628479.html

   Important: The CYD version does not work.


 - M5Stack Dial V1.1 (1 piece)

   https://shop.m5stack.com/products/m5stack-dial-v1-1?srsltid=AfmBOorVdA7JWBmnLc-y1Z5nxQ-gy96z45HtuLObhLn81BahLKdarn4e


 - Waveshare PN532 NFC HAT NFC Reader

   https://amzn.eu/d/00uf6aqM

   Important: Please use only this model. I tested several NFC readers, and this one proved to be the most reliable.

   Even the original Elechouse NFC Module V3 did not perform satisfactorily. Some AliExpress Elechouse clones did not work at all, while others only worked with limitations.


 - USB-C panel-mount connector (1 piece)

   https://amzn.eu/d/0a2qntre


 - M2 × 6 pan head screws and nuts (2 pieces each, For mounting the USB port)


 - M3 × 4 pan head screws (4 pieces, For mounting the display)


 - M3 × 6 flanged pan head screws (4 pieces, For securing the bottom cover)


 - M2 × 8 hex socket screws (4 pieces, For assembling the enclosure)


 - Suction cup

   https://amzn.eu/d/0bF8AD8d


 - 4-pin header strip

   https://amzn.eu/d/09LT9mn4
